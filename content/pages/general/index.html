Against a clear background, it should appear in black typeface. - A greyscale version of the logo is also available. ### Download our logo [Download logos from the Intranet](https://tsc.ebi.ac.uk/article/logos) ### EMBL-EBI official colours EMBL-EBI uses specific colours on screen (RGB) and in print (CMYK in compliance with ISO 2846, using HKS or PANTONE defined colours).
