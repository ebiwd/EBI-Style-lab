{{{edit-button 'general_about.html' 'section'}}} {{#markdown}} ### Where can I find icons? The Web Guidelines Committee (wgc@ebi.ac.uk) has put together five icon fonts, so there are many to choose from. You can access these fonts via the web guidelines. ### Where can I find images to use on my web page? The External Relations team (comms@ebi.ac.uk) is working on setting up a visual asset library to make it easier to share images throughout EMBL-EBI. If you are looking for pictures of EMBL-EBI or of the Genome Campus, you can use any of the photos available on the images page. You can find different versions of our logos on the [Logos and branding](https://tsc.ebi.ac.uk/article/logos) page. If you require photos of a specific species (e.g., a silverback gorilla and no other), we encourage you to speak with the Ensembl team because they have already done a lot of work to secure many of these images. ### I'm stuck and need help designing or writing content for my web pages. Who do I contact? Contact the Web Guidelines Committee, it's a large and diverse group of people who are happy to help. If you have technical issue, post to the EBI Visual Framework issue queue. {{/markdown}}
